Quick Food Tracker

Simple nutrition tracking for iOS – fast, private, no cloud required.

Your data stays on your device. Quick Food Tracker does not store any data on its own servers.

Features

  • Quickly log nutrients and save them to Apple Health
  • AI-powered food recognition (Google Gemini & ChatGPT)
  • Widgets and Live Activities for the lock screen
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly nutrition analytics
  • Available in English and German

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored exclusively on your iPhone. If you have iCloud enabled, data is synced to your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your data at any point.

How does AI food recognition work?

You provide your own API key for Google Gemini or ChatGPT. Requests are sent directly from your device to the AI provider – without routing through our servers. Everything that is sent is always visible in the dialog before submission.

Does the app work without AI?

Yes. AI features are entirely optional. You can always enter nutrition values manually.

How do I delete my data?

All data can be deleted at any time directly within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device.

The app does not appear in Apple Health – what should I do?

Go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Quick Food Tracker, and make sure write permissions are enabled for the desired nutrients.

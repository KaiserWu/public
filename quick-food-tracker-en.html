Privacy Policy

QuickFoodTracker App

Last updated: September 2025
Important Notice: The QuickFoodTracker app does not collect data in our own cloud systems. All your data remains under your complete control.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The QuickFoodTracker app was developed with a "Privacy by Design" approach. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers or cloud services.

2. Local Data Storage

All data you enter in the app is exclusively:

  • Stored locally on your iPhone - Data remains within the app on your device
  • Stored in your personal iCloud - If you have enabled iCloud synchronization, data is stored in your own iCloud account

We never have access to this data at any time.

3. AI Services and Data Processing

When you use AI features in the app:

  • Your requests are sent directly to the AI service provider you have configured
  • Processing is performed exclusively by the respective AI provider
  • We act merely as an intermediary and have no access to this communication
  • All AI requests and prompts are displayed transparently in dialog windows - You can always see what data is being sent to the AI service
  • The privacy policies of the respective AI service provider apply

4. No Data Transmission to Us

QuickFoodTracker does not transmit any user data, food entries, preferences, or other personal information to our servers. Your privacy is completely protected.

5. Information About AI Service Providers

For detailed information about data processing by AI services, please contact the respective AI service provider directly. Each provider has their own privacy policies that apply to the processing of your requests.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, you always have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete all data at any time through the app settings
  • You can disable iCloud synchronization
  • You can uninstall the app without data loss (data remains in iCloud if enabled)

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please feel free to contact us. For specific questions about data processing by AI services, please contact the respective provider directly.
